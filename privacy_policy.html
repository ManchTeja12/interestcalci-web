Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

Interest Calculator ("we", "us", or "the App") is a financial calculation application designed to help users calculate simple interest, compound interest, EMIs, loan details, savings goals, and other finance-related values. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account or log in.
  • User Input Data: Loan amounts, borrower details, interest rates, time periods, savings goals, and calculation results you save within the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, IP address, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, buttons clicked, screens visited, and interactions inside the App.
  • Subscription and Purchase Data: If you purchase premium features using Google Play Billing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s calculators and financial tools.
  • Save and manage your borrower records, loan history, and calculations.
  • Authenticate your login and maintain your account.
  • Send important updates, notifications, or reminders (if enabled).
  • Fix bugs, improve performance, and analyze how the App is used.
  • Support premium features and process subscription purchases.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard measures. We use encryption where applicable and restrict unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Analytics tools, crash-reporting services, or hosting providers.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • With Your Permission: If you choose to export or share calculation results.

5. Google Play Billing

If you buy a subscription or premium feature, Google Play may collect purchase-related information according to its own Privacy Policy. We do not store sensitive payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Billing
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics
  • Cloud hosting or database services

These services follow their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is found, we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time.

9. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to the data we collect about you
  • Correction or deletion of your data
  • Account deletion

You can contact us using the details below to exercise these rights.
